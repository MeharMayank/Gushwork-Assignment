Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about HDPE pipes and our services.

What is the lifespan of HDPE pipes? +

HDPE pipes have a service life of 50+ years under normal operating conditions. They are designed to withstand various environmental stresses and maintain structural integrity over decades.

Can HDPE pipes be used for hot water applications? +

Yes, HDPE pipes can handle temperatures up to 80¬∞C (176¬∞F). For continuous hot water applications, proper pressure derating should be considered based on temperature.

What joining methods are available for HDPE pipes? +

HDPE pipes can be joined using butt fusion welding, electrofusion welding, and mechanical fittings. Fusion welding provides the strongest, leak-proof joints.

Are HDPE pipes suitable for drinking water? +

Absolutely! HDPE pipes are certified for potable water applications. They don't leach chemicals, maintain water quality, and are approved by water authorities worldwide.

What maintenance is required for HDPE pipes? +

HDPE pipes require minimal maintenance due to their corrosion resistance and smooth interior. Periodic pressure testing and visual inspections are recommended.
